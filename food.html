Food is a very big part of my life. Whether I'm making it or eating it, I love it. My mom always says "I live to eat, instead of eating to live." Two of my favorite pasttimes are cooking and baking, especially for my friends and family. My brother is my biggest fan when it comes to my baked goods. Some of my favorite things to make are homemade poptarts, berry or apple crisp, and a special pasta dish I make fo my brother.
